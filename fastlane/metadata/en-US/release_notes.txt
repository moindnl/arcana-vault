What's new in v2.2

7 power zones — the app now uses the full Coggan/Allen model (Z1–Z7), including Anaerobic and Neuromuscular. Zone badge and formula sheet updated accordingly.

Race templates — tap the flag icon to pre-fill distance and target power for common events: Gran Fondo 100/150, Ironman bike leg, 70.3 bike leg, Cyclosportive, Criterium.

Heat warning — slide the temperature above 32°C and a reminder appears to drink more. Tap × to dismiss for that session.

Pack list confetti — check off your last item and something small happens. No spoilers.

Launch screen — the app now has a proper startup screen instead of a white flash.

Bug fix — custom products with high carbs per unit (e.g. Maurten 100, bars) were showing "no solid food needed" in the fueling schedule. Fixed.

A bunch of color and contrast fixes, especially in dark mode.
