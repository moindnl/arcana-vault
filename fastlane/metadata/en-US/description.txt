Stop guessing. Start fueling.

bonkproof calculates your exact carbohydrate and fluid targets based on your FTP and ride data — so you arrive at the finish line strong, not empty.

Enter your weight and FTP once. Then for each ride, add your planned power and duration. bonkproof gives you:

• Carb targets per hour (g/h) — calibrated to your training zone
• Fluid needs per hour — adjusted for weight, sweat rate, and heat
• A fueling schedule — what to eat and drink, and when
• A packing checklist — so nothing gets forgotten

Built for cyclists who take performance seriously. No account needed, no subscription, no noise — just the numbers that matter.
